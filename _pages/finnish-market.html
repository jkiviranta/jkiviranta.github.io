Finnish Electricity Market

This page gives an overview of the Finnish electricity market.

Wholesale market

Auction format Uniform price auction
Possibility of bilateral trade Firms can have PPAs bypassing the wholesale market
Number of bidding zones 1
Connections to other countries Sweden and Estonia
Private grid Lower level grid is privatized and operates with regulated tariffs.
Transmission system operator Fingrid
Market place Nord Pool

Retail market

Liberalized retail market Yes
Dynamic price or real-time price contracts Available everywhere.
Outside option Indefinite contract with a fixed price that changes every month.
The contract is offered by a local retailer.
Electricity market authority Energiavirasto
